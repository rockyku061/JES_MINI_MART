❓ FAQ / How It Works

Find answers to common questions and learn how JES Student Mart works.

Frequently Asked Questions

🧑‍🎓 How do I log in as a student?

Click Login on the home page, enter your Email, Registration Number, and Password, and you will be redirected to your dashboard.

📦 How do I add a product?

From the Student Dashboard, click Add Product. Fill in the product name, price, description, and upload an image.

🛒 How do I add products to my cart?

Browse products on the dashboard and click Add to Cart. The product will automatically be saved in your My Cart page.

📜 Where can I see my past orders?

Your previous purchases are listed in the Purchase History page.

💬 How can I contact the seller?

Open the product detail page and click Chat with Seller to discuss the product.

❌ What does Sold Out mean?

If a seller marks a product as Sold Out, the product will be removed from the dashboard and will no longer be available for purchase.

⚙️ Can I update my profile?

Yes, go to Profile Settings to change your details.

❓ How do I log out?

Click the Logout button in the top navigation bar.
